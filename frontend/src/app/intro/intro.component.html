What is this tool for?

Welcome to the GenAI reflection tool. The aim of this tool is to stimulate reflection on the impact of generative AI on academic teaching. Students can use generative AI for almost all assignments. The question whether assessment forms need to be adapted as a consequence of this, depends on the intended learning outcomes that the assignments test. This is why this tool aims to make an explicit link between assessment forms and learning outcomes. Wherever the use of generative AI is considered problematic, the tool provides alternative assessment forms, or stimulates you to consider changing the learning outcomes.

This tool consists of three subsequent steps:

  • Step 1: Learning outcomes analysis clarify and map your course goals
  • Step 2: Assessment forms assess whether the use of generative AI in assignments negatively impacts the learning outcomes
  • Step 3: Ideas for integrating generative AI in your course explore how generative AI can be used to enhance your course

Before we begin...

Please provide the name of your course and the name of the lecturers. This will personalize the final report.
