What is this tool for?

Welcome to the GenAI reflection tool. This tool is designed to reflect on the impact of GenAI on the learning goals of individual courses. It works best if you run this tool with a particular course in mind (and to have the course manual ready). The tool will guide you through some steps (see below) that challenge you to reflect on the validity of the intended learning outcomes of the course and the effectiveness of the assignments to test these. Once finished, you will be offered a report with an overview of the outcomes.

You can run the tool as many times and for as many courses as you like. We do not store or save the information you enter in this tool.

How the tool works

This tool consists of four subsequent steps:

  • Step 1: Learning outcomes analysis clarify and map your course goals.
  • Step 2: Assessments assess whether the use of generative AI in assignments negatively impacts the learning outcomes.
  • Step 3: Rethinking courses in times of GenAI explore how generative AI can be used to enhance your course.
  • Step 4: Report after finishing the tool you can download a report with the most important outcomes.

Before we begin...

Please provide the name of your course and the name of the lecturers. This will personalize the final report.
